Queen as Ziggy Stardust

This image was created as a tribute to the Queens’ diamond jubilee, and shows the Queen dressed as David Bowie

Well Hung Lover

The man in the suit is said to depict the womans’ husband. Her husband is accusing her of adultery and is looking out of the window in search for the man she is having an affair with.

The Girl with the Pierced Eardrum

Is adapted from Vermeer’s The Girl with the Pearl Earring. The pearl earing in this drawing is replaced by a security alarm. It was painted in October 2014 in Bristol Harbourside.
